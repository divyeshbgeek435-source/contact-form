## What Information We Collect

### From Your Shopify Store

When you install our app, we collect:
- Your shop name and email
- Your shop ID
- Access to your customers, orders, and products (as needed for the app to work)

### From Form Submissions

When customers fill out forms on your store, we collect:
- Names, emails, phone numbers
- All information entered in form fields (text, numbers, checkboxes, etc.)
- When the form was submitted

### Technical Information

We also collect:
- How you use the app
- Your device and browser type
- Basic usage logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Store and show form submissions
- Let you export data to CSV
- Help you search and filter submissions
- Show you statistics (total submissions, daily counts, etc.)
- Improve the app
- Fix technical problems
- Answer your questions

## How We Keep Your Data Safe

- We store your data on secure servers
- We use encryption to protect data in transit
- Only authorized people can access your data
- We keep your data as long as you use the app

**Note:** No system is 100% secure, but we do our best to protect your information.
